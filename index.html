Catholic Couples Retreat: Renewing Love in Christ

Step away from the busyness of life and take time to nurture the sacred bond of your marriage. This retreat offers couples a grace-filled opportunity to rest, reconnect, and rediscover the beauty of God's plan for your vocation.

Through inspiring talks, guided prayer, and practical workshops, you and your spouse will deepen your communication, strengthen your spiritual life together, and rekindle the joy of your commitment. The retreat will also include Mass, Eucharistic Adoration, Confession, and time for personal reflection as well as fellowship with other Catholic couples walking the same journey.
